### 2. **uvm/cpu_driver_c.sv** (164 lines) — CPU Driver
- **Read protocol** (`drv_rd_trans`):
  1. `@(posedge clk)`: assert `cpu_rd=1`, drive `addr_bus_cpu_lv1`
  2. `@(posedge clk)`: assert `cpu_rden=1`
  3. Wait for `data_in_bus_cpu_lv1` posedge OR 110-cycle timeout
  4. `@(posedge clk)`: deassert `cpu_rd=0`, release addr to Z
  5. `@(posedge clk)`: deassert `cpu_rden=0`, disable fork
  6. Wait for `negedge data_in_bus_cpu_lv1` or posedge clk, then one more posedge clk

- **Write protocol** (`drv_wr_trans`):
  1. `@(posedge clk)`: assert `cpu_wr=1`, drive `addr_bus_cpu_lv1`, drive `data_bus_cpu_lv1_reg`
  2. `@(posedge clk)`: assert `cpu_wren=1`
  3. Wait for `cpu_wr_done` posedge OR 110-cycle timeout
  4. `@(posedge clk)`: deassert `cpu_wr=0`, release addr/data to Z
  5. `@(posedge clk)`: deassert `cpu_wren=0`, disable fork
  6. Wait for `negedge cpu_wr_done` or posedge clk, then one more posedge clk
